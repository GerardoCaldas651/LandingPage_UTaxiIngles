About Us

UTaxi

UTaxi is a mobile platform that allows university students to connect with other university students to take trips to the university, that is, students with cars can share theirs with others who live near them to go to university.

These people can charge a ticket to each student they take through the platform, be able to select the people they want to take and so they have their seat reserved.

Dynamic Solutions Impacts

Dynamic Solutions Impacts is a platform company focused on mobile devices, however, we also develop platforms for other types of devices, but mainly we target mobile devices since they are the most used devices currently worldwide and are more frequent in young people which is our target audience.

We want to provide solutions to people's daily problems in a safe and effective way, for this we propose different project ideas among the members of the company to know which is the one that best solves people's day-to-day problems and choose the best option to develop.

Our Service

Our service offers a safe mobilization for university students. This offers security both to them and to their valuables such as cell phones, laptops, etc.

We use the university database, which allows us to identify the students who offer the taxi service. We also verify that the user does not have a criminal record and is not at academic risk. We also take care of the transfer of the client to safe areas of the district and that they are guaranteed proximity to their home without revealing their personal data, since our service consists of giving the client a "ride", not leaving it at the door of their house.

Our service works with students enrolled in the, who have a vehicle and a valid driver's license. UTaxi has access to this data, this guarantees an effective identification in case of problems.
